Duties and Responsibilities

 

The goal of this role is to gradually achieve superior customer engagement and intimacy, website traffic and revenue by strategically exploiting all aspects of the social media marketing roadmap. You should have a solid understanding of how each social media channel works and how to optimize content so that it is engaging on those channels. You are responsible for joining relevant conversations on behalf of the brand and “soft selling” the product by providing support to current and prospective customers.

Our ideal candidate is a social media enthusiast with experience in copywriting, graphic design, and digital content creation. To be successful in this role, you must have a creative mind, passion for marketing  and engagement, excellent time management skills and be eager to keep abreast of social media trends, industry innovations and relevant software.

We are seeking a motivated individual, who is passionate about social media management, to join our team of marketing professionals as an Audience Engagement Associate. In this role, you will help develop and execute global social media campaigns, creating shareable content and deliverables to support greater brand awareness and engagement.
