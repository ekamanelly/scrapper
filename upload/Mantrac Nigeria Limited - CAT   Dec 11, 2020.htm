Job Requirements

 

Improve quality in line with Mantracspolicy to exceed customers expectations always.

Perform other HR functions as may be required by the Human Resources Manager.

Relieve of HR personnel as the need arises

Establish and operate link to payroll system (as a backup for the Compensation and Benefits Unit).

Create regular reports and presentations on HR metrics.

Develop and maintain organizational communications such as bulletin boards and newsletters to ensure employees have knowledge of training and development events and resources.

Assist with the investigation and issuance of warning letters, maintain disciplinary records, and schedule for all staff.

Implement employee movement requirements such as Leave, Transfer, Cessations and others.

Assist with managing the process of Employee Opinion Survey and implement Action Plans as required.

Ensure that Expatriate related documents are promptly obtained/updated, while relevant returns are made to the appropriate authorities.

Co-ordinate the implementation of the companys Performance Management System.

Implement Recruitment, Selection and On-boarding processes and maintain database in this regard.
